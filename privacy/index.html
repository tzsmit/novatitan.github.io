Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Introduction

At Nova Titan Systems, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website, services, and digital products.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number (when provided through forms or bookings).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and limited analytics data to improve site performance.
  • Service Data: Details provided during consultations, quotes, or project requests.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and manage our services.
  • To communicate with you about inquiries, projects, or support.
  • To improve website performance and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to safeguard your data, including secure storage practices, encryption where appropriate, and limiting access to authorized personnel only.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Information may be shared only when legally required or with trusted third parties (such as booking platforms) strictly to deliver our services.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us at info@novatitan.net to exercise these rights.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.
